Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see yours here, reach out anytime.

Why is the Business Starter plan cheaper than other website options?
Because your website is hosted within The Marketplace platform rather than a standalone hosting environment. This shared infrastructure lets us pass the savings on to you — while still delivering a professional, fully custom website.
Can I upgrade from Business Starter to Business Pro later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade to Business Pro at any time to get your own custom domain and professional email addresses. There's no penalty or restart required — we handle the migration for you.
What's the difference between monthly and yearly billing on Business Pro?
Monthly billing is $100/month with no commitment — cancel anytime. Yearly billing is $899 paid upfront for 12 months, saving you $289 compared to paying monthly. Most businesses choose yearly to lock in the savings and simplify their billing.
Is hosting included in all plans?
Yes — hosting is included in all monthly plans (Business Starter and Business Pro). For one-time packages like Starter and Growth, we assist with setup and can recommend hosting solutions that suit your needs.
What does "content updates" mean in the monthly plans?
It means you can request changes to your website content — such as updating text, images, hours, pricing, or contact information — and we'll make those changes for you as part of your monthly plan. No extra charges for standard updates.
What social media platforms do you manage?
We work with all major platforms including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and LinkedIn. When you get in touch, we'll discuss which platforms are most relevant for your business and audience.
Can I cancel a monthly plan at any time?
Yes. All monthly plans are rolling with no lock-in contracts. You can cancel at any time, and your website will remain live through the end of your current billing period.
Can I add extras to any plan?
Yes — all add-ons (extra pages, SEO, Google Business setup, website revamp, and maintenance) can be added to any plan at any time, either at the start of your project or later down the track.
