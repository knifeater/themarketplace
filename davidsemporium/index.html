About Us

Your One-Stop Creative Destination

David's Emporium is a versatile, family-owned New Zealand retail business operating as a variety store, discount store, and craft supplier. We specialise in arts and crafts, fancy dress, sewing supplies, hardware and homewares.

You'll find a truckload of items at our stores, with everything you need to bring your creative projects to life. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff look forward to providing ideas and helping you find exactly what you need.
